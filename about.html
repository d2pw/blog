We offer in-depth knowledge and expertise in analysis of data security risk. Our experience working with local businesses, governmental agencies and Fortune 500 companies gives us the ability to help organizations of all sizes better perceive risk and take the necessary steps to mitigate them. We have assisted organizations develop and implement solutions to manage personal identifiable information (PII), making sure proper procedures are in place to ensure compliance with local data protection agencies such as the CNIL in France and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC)

It is our belief that well trained security-conscious staff creates a positive environment where people feel empowered in their mission to contribute to an organizations policy of maintaining the confidentiality, integrity and availability of its data.

We reduce your security and privacy risks by:

  • Creating the foundation of your risk management program
  • Making sure appropriate measures are in place to protect your data
  • Training staff to identify risks and to respond professionally
  • Developing procedures to mitigate potential incidents
